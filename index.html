XY Plane Precision: FDM & Resin

High-resolution resin printing for fine feature display models and SLA engineering parts. FDM for rapid, large-format prototyping in engineering polymers. Any footprint from 10mm to 1000mm.

Z-Axis Strength: SLS Nylon

Isotropic nylon PA12 via SLS and MJF for functional parts with equal strength in all three axes. No support structures, no weak layer lines. Production-equivalent mechanical properties.

Full-Volume Metal: DMLS

Steel, titanium, aluminum, and Inconel DMLS printing for the most demanding full-volume metal geometry. Any internal channel, any lattice, any undercut — in production metal alloys.
